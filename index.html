Welcome to WELD101 – Welding Fundamentals

Designed for the student who does not intend to become employed in the welding industry but desires a background in welding for general education purposes and/or as a supportive course for his/her studies such as automotive mechanics, auto body repair, air conditioning and refrigeration, artistic sculpturing, etc. Instruction is given in the basic techniques of safely operating the Shielded Metal Arc Welding (SMAW) and oxyacetylene welding processes. (CSU, AVC)

Complete required safety work before participating in hands-on lab work. Check Canvas announcements regularly for instructor updates, reminders, and schedule changes.
